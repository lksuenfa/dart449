Artist statement

This website has been created by Leanne Kate Suen Fa in the context of course DART449 at Concordia University in Winter 2022.

In this project, I wanted to approach the food waste crisis through an activist web intervention challenging the concept of food waste reduction efforts focusing mainly on consumer efforts and donations, which is the approach adopted by the Canadian Federal government. By adopting both an informative, nearly infographical approach to web design in addition to playfully staged interactions and illustrations, I wanted capture the childish wonderment of playing with interactive books and bring the reader away from the expectations mainstream commercial web design. This unique experience aims to spark joy and interest in the reader rather than push him in a dramatic spiral of pessimistic facts on sustainability which are often seen in interventions aiming to mobilize the public.
